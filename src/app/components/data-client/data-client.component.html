Dave is a director/photographer living in Los Angeles. He got his start shooting album covers for 50 Cent, Chris Brown, Three 6 Mafia, Soulja Boy, etc. This led to shooting national ad campaigns over the past decade.

Recently, Dave has been collaborating with the top agencies and brands, directing TVC’s across the globe that combine his love of adventure, people, fashion, and cars. With a background in skating and snowboarding, he strives to tell stories that are fast-paced and energetic, always keeping the camera moving.

Dave wrote/directed his first narrative feature film, FLYING CARS, which premiered at the 2019 Dances with Films Festival in Los Angeles. It’s available worldwide on Apple TV, Amazon, and Google Play.

Recent clients: Share Now, VW, Mercedes, Renault, Dodge, FIAT, Chrysler, Jeep, Alfa Romeo, Ford
